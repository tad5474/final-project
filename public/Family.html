Activities for Families

About

The goal of the Penn State Exploration Guide is to help anyone visiting or living in the State College area to find things that they like to do in the area. We know that their are a large variety of tastes and preferences, so we have broken up our ideas into different groups. This way no matter who you are or what you are interested in, our site should be able to help you find something you like in the State College area. Our categories are Adventure, Elderly, Adult, Family, Student, and Seasonal but feel free to look through any category as many activities are enjoyable for people of all kinds!

Downtown Destinations

If you're a fan of golf or want to try something new outdoors, the Penn State Golf Courses have what you need. There are two courses to choose from as well as a driving range. If you are dedicated to the sport and you want to bring your own clubs, there are golf carts for rent- or if you want to try something new, you can also rent clubs. Tee times are all day and you can find the right price within your budget by selecting a specific time and the number of holes you plan to play. Visit their website for more info.

Game Day Ideas

The Berkey Creamery is a staple of most people's visit to campus. With over 100 flavors and 32 flavors daily, there are so many delicious options to pick from. As a favorite stop from children to alum alike, there are flavors and sweets for everyone. The creamery also provides other campus made diary products such as cheeses, milks, spreads, yogurts, and more! Make sure you come hungry because the scoops are going to be the size of your head!

Campus Attractions

The Nittany Lion Shrine is a fun place to document your trip to Penn State. With a lovely treed background and lights at night, you can be sure to get a photo of your whole famiy showing their lion pride! The statue is located next to Rec Hall and the Kern Building. There is parking nearby, as well as a convient bus stops.

Nearby Options

Penn's Cave is a fun outting for any age, but show your kids some historical wonders of the Pennsylvannia landscapes and caves. Only a 25 min drive from State College, you and your family can fill your afternoon with a exploritory boat ride and the wildlife park! Visit their website for more info.
